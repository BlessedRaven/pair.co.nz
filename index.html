Compound Analysis

PAIR’s AI dissects the molecular makeup of plants and resources—detecting alkaloids, flavonoids, terpenes, and minerals in seconds. Identify caffeine in wild coffee beans, salicylic acid in willow bark, or quartz in a rock sample. Perfect for botanists, chemists, and survivalists seeking precision.

Medicinal Breakthroughs

Discover pharmaceutical goldmines in your backyard. PAIR flags compounds like morphine in poppies or anti-cancer agents in yew trees, providing dosage guidelines and preparation methods. It’s a game-changer for herbalists, doctors, and remote communities needing accessible healthcare.

Resource Optimization

Turn raw materials into solutions. PAIR identifies silica in horsetail for glassmaking, tannins in oak bark for leather tanning, or iron oxides in clay for pigments. Crafters, engineers, and off-grid pioneers can maximize every resource with real-time data.

Ecosystem Guardianship

Monitor plant health and environmental conditions. PAIR detects pesticide residues, fungal infections, or nutrient deficiencies in crops, alongside soil pH and water purity. Farmers, conservationists, and researchers can protect ecosystems with actionable insights.
